TrailTales User Manual
1. Introduction
Welcome to TrailTales, your digital companion for documenting life’s journey. TrailTales allows you to pin your memories to a map, organize them by date, and share your adventures with friends. This manual will guide you through the features of the application.

2. Getting Started
2.1 Creating an Account
Launch the application.
Click on "Sign Up".
Enter your email, username, and a secure password.
Select an Avatar Color to represent you on the map and in chats.
2.2 Logging In
Enter your registered email and password.
Click "Login" to access your Dashboard.
3. Managing Friends
Based on the Friend System logic.

3.1 Finding People
You can find other users by their username or email address.

Navigate to the Friends/Search section in the Sidebar.
Type a name or email into the search bar.
The system will display a list of users matching your query (excluding yourself).
3.2 Sending a Friend Request
Locate the user you wish to connect with in the search results.
Click the "Add Friend" button.
Note: You cannot send a request to yourself or to someone who is already your friend. If a request is already pending, you will be notified.
3.3 Accepting or Rejecting Requests
When someone sends you a request, it will appear in your Pending Requests tab.

Go to the Friend Requests panel.
You will see the sender's username and avatar.
To Accept: Click the Checkmark (✓) button. You will immediately become friends, and they will appear in your contact list.
To Reject: Click the Cross (X) button. The request will be removed.
3.4 Viewing Your Friend List
Open the Friends Sidebar.
You will see a list of all your current friends.
Unread Messages: If a friend has sent you a message you haven't read yet, a number counter will appear next to their name indicating how many unread messages you have.
4. Journaling & Mapping
4.1 Creating a Memory
Map Mode: Click anywhere on the map to drop a pin.
Dashboard Mode: Click the "+" (Add Memory) button.
Details:
Title: Give your memory a name.
Description: Write about your experience.
Date: Select when this happened.
Media: Upload photos or videos related to the memory.
Privacy: Choose if this memory is Private (only you) or Public (friends can see).
Click Save. A pin will appear on the map at that location.
4.2 Viewing Memories
Map View: Browse the map. Click on any pin to open the Memory View Modal and see the photos and story.
Calendar View: Switch to the Calendar tab to see your memories organized by date. Dates with entries will be highlighted.
5. Social Features
5.1 Chatting
Click on a friend's name in your Friend List.
A chat window will open.
Type your message and hit Send.
You can discuss specific memories or plan your next trip together.
5.2 Notifications
You will receive toast notifications (pop-ups) for:

New Friend Requests.
Successful uploads of memories.
Errors (e.g., if you try to add a friend who doesn't exist).
6. Troubleshooting & FAQ
Q: Why can't I send a friend request?

A: Check if you have already sent one (it might be pending). Also, ensure you aren't already friends with that user.
Q: My map isn't loading my location.

A: Ensure you have granted Location Permissions to your browser. Refresh the page and click "Allow" when prompted.
Q: How do I delete a memory?

A: Open the memory details and click the Trash Icon. You will be asked to confirm before it is permanently deleted.
7. Privacy & Safety
Location Data: Your location is only used to place pins on the map.
Content Visibility: Memories marked as "Private
" are encrypted and visible only to you.

Blocking: If a user is bothering you, you can reject their friend requests. (Future updates will include a Block feature).
